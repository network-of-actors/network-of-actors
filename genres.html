Statistics

Have you ever wondered how much it costs to make a sci-fi movie compared to a romantic comedy? Or wondered whether the actors are particularly young or old in movies of a specific genre? Don't worry - you don't have to wonder anymore! With the following statistical comparison of the genres, all your questions will be answered.
The bar plots below compares the following attributes for all 9 genres:
  • The average estimated budget (cost of making) of a movie
  • The average age of the cast at the time where the movie was filmed
  • The average size of the main cast of a movie
  • The average sentiment score of a movie (based on the plot synopsis and key words)
